# Mental Health Analytics Power BI Project

## Overview
The **Mental Health Analytics** project is part of the Power BI Projects Repository. This project focuses on analyzing data related to mental health trends, workplace productivity, and employee satisfaction. The dashboard provides actionable insights to address mental health challenges and their impact on organizational performance.

## Features

### Dashboards
- **Mental Health and Productivity Analysis:**
  - Average Mental Health Score: 3.27 (Goal: 5, -34.67%)
  - Productivity Score: 49.20 (Goal: 89, -44.72%)
  - Absenteeism Rate: 22.24%
  - Turnover Rate: 33.33%

- **Demographic Insights:**
  - Gender-wise mental health trends, with Agender and Female groups showing higher mental health scores.
  - Age group analysis identifying middle-aged employees with lower mental health scores and the 50-60 age group with the highest turnover rate.

- **Department-Wise Analysis:**
  - Engineering Department has the lowest mental health score.
  - Sales Department demonstrates the highest productivity relative to mental health.
  - Business Development shows the lowest productivity-to-mental health ratio.

- **Quarterly Trends:**
  - Quarter 3 has the lowest mental health scores and highest absenteeism rates.

### Visualizations
- **Interactive Visuals:**
  - Heatmaps for department-wise productivity and mental health comparisons.
  - Line charts showing productivity and mental health trends over time.
  - Filters and slicers for age, gender, department, and job satisfaction levels.

### Datasets
- **Excel-based Data Sources:**
  - Data includes mental health scores, productivity metrics, absenteeism, turnover, and demographic details.

- **SQL Integration:**
  - Data preprocessing and transformation to ensure accuracy and performance.

## Key Metrics
- **Top Insights:**
  - Satisfied employees show lower absenteeism and turnover rates.
  - Employees aged 50-60 exhibit the highest turnover rates.
  - Business Development has the lowest productivity compared to mental health.

## Structure

The project is organized as follows:

- **Mental Health/:**
  - Contains the Power BI dashboard file (`Mental_Health_Analytics.pbix`).

- **Datasets/:**

## Technologies Used

- **Power BI:** Data visualization and reporting.
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** For advanced calculations and metrics.
