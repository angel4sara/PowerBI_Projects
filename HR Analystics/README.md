# HR Analytics Power BI Project

## Overview
The **HR Analytics** project is part of the Power BI Projects Repository. This project focuses on analyzing key HR metrics to provide actionable insights for workforce management. The dashboard utilizes data analytics, advanced DAX expressions, and interactive visualizations to highlight trends and inform decision-making in human resources.

## Features

### Dashboards
- **Attrition Analysis:**
  - Total Employees: 1,470
  - Attrition: 237 employees (16.1%)
  - Insights into attrition by factors such as age, salary, years of employment, and job roles.

- **Employee Demographics Analysis:**
  - Average Age: 37
  - Gender distribution and breakdown by department.
  - Diversity metrics across education levels and job roles.

- **Job Satisfaction Insights:**
  - Satisfaction levels categorized by job roles.
  - Highlights areas of low satisfaction to aid HR interventions.

- **Salary and Experience Trends:**
  - Attrition by salary ranges: Up to 5K, 5K-10K, and beyond.
  - Correlation between years of employment and attrition.

### Visualizations
- **Interactive Visuals:**
  - Charts and heatmaps for attrition by age group, job roles, and salary.
  - Dynamic slicers and filters for deeper exploration of HR data.

### Datasets
- **Excel-based Data Sources:**
  - Employee demographic and performance data, including age, gender, department, salary, education, and years of employment.

- **SQL Integration:**
  - Data preprocessing and aggregation using SQL for efficient analysis.

## Key Metrics
- **Attrition by Job Role:**

- **Attrition by Age Group:**
